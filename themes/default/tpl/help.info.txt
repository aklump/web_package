Show all package info, or specific by arg

usage: web_package info [<key>]
       web_package i [<key>]

For some keys you may omit the 'info' as the first argument as a shorthand method.

The optional arguments for show are, these may be
      version | v
      name
      templates [<template>]

To see the contents of a template type:

    web_package info templates drupal
